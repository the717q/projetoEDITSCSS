Frequently Asked Questions

We gathered the most asked questions, so we can accurately help you as fast as possible.

How much time does it take to deliver the product

If you are buying from Europe, it takes usually 3 to 5 business days, outside Europe it can take up to 10 business days

Can i buy more than one product?

You can buy as many products as you want. They will all be delivered in one shipment

If i am not satistied, can i return the product?

You have 10 days to return to product, as long as it is not damaged.

How and when does the refund takes place?

The refund takes place as soon as our department receives the product and we process the refund into your bank account.

Do you deliver to outside Europe?

We deliver to all parts of the world, but the shipment costs and delivery dates may differ from country to country
